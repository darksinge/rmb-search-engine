         DEPOSIT OF PUBLIC FUNDS           GENERAL SESSION      STATE OF UTAH      Chief Sponsor   Adam Gardiner      Senate Sponsor    Lincoln Fillmore             LONG TITLE      General Description            This bill modifies provisions relating to deposits of public funds by a political       subdivision.       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   amends the time frame requirement for an officer of a political subdivision to       deposit public funds  and                  makes technical corrections.
