General Description             This bill amends provisions related to financial institutions under the jurisdiction of the       department.       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   modifies definitions                   permits the delegation of powers and duties under certain circumstances                   changes the supervisor of trust to the supervisor of holding companies                   modifies restrictions on acquisition of institutions and holding companies  and                  makes technical changes. In addition to the supervisors under Sections         through        .  and               . , the commissioner may appoint additional supervisors as necessary. The salaries of the employees of the department shall be fixed in accordance with        salary and merit standards adopted by the Division of Finance and are payable in the same        manner as the salaries of other state employees.
