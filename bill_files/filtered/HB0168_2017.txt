This part is known as  Kindergarten Supplemental Enrichment Program.             Section  .  Section   A         is enacted to read               A        . Definitions.            As used in this part                   Board  means the State Board of Education.                  Kindergarten supplemental enrichment program  means a program to improve the       academic competency of kindergarten students that              a   meets the criteria described in Section   A                      b   receives funding from a grant program described in Section   A          and             c   is administered by a public school.
