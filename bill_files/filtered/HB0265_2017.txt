Henderson             LONG TITLE      General Description            This bill modifies provisions relating to motor vehicle safety inspection programs.       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   repeals the requirement that certain vehicles obtain a safety inspection certificate in       order to be registered and to operate on a highway  and                  makes technical changes.       Money Appropriated in this Bill             None       Other Special Clauses             This bill provides a special effective date.
