         HOMEOWNERS ASSOCIATION REVISIONS           GENERAL SESSION      STATE OF UTAH      Chief Sponsor   John Knotwell      Senate Sponsor                            LONG TITLE      General Description            This bill amends provisions related to condominium and community associations.       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   provides that a condominium or community association shall comply with certain       requirements before bringing a legal action against a declarant related to a period of       declarant control or period of administrative control.
