         PRESIDENTIAL PRIMARY AMENDMENTS           GENERAL SESSION      STATE OF UTAH      Chief Sponsor   Patrice M. Arent      Senate Sponsor                            LONG TITLE      General Description            This bill requires that the state hold a presidential primary election every four years.       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   requires the Legislature to fund a presidential primary election  and                  requires a political party to participate in, and use the results of, a presidential       primary election to determine the political party s nominee for president of the       United States.       Money Appropriated in this Bill             This bill appropriates                   to the Office of the Governor   Office of the Lieutenant Governor, as a one time       appropriation                        from the General Fund,     ,   , subject to intent language stating that the       appropriation be spent for a presidential primary election  and                  to the Office of the Governor   Office of the Lieutenant Governor, as an ongoing,       nonlapsing appropriation                        from the General Fund,     ,   , subject to intent language stating that the       appropriation be spent for a presidential primary election.       Other Special Clauses             None       Utah Code Sections Affected        ENACTS               A      , Utah Code Annotated                    Be it enacted by the Legislature of the state of Utah             Section  .  Section   A       is enacted to read               A      . Presidential primary required.                 A presidential primary election shall be held each year in which a presidential       election will be held.                 A registered political party that wishes to nominate a presidential candidate for the       general election shall participate in a presidential primary election conducted under this section       and is required to use the results of the election exclusively in selecting the political party s       delegates to the political party s quadrennial national nominating convention at which the       political party selects a nominee for the office of president of the United States.                 The Legislature shall appropriate sufficient funds to administer each presidential       primary election conducted under this section.            Section  .  Appropriation.
