         SCHOOL BOARD MIDTERM REPLACEMENT PROCESS           GENERAL SESSION      STATE OF UTAH      Chief Sponsor   Susan Pulsipher      Senate Sponsor                            LONG TITLE      General Description            This bill addresses the process by which a midterm vacancy is filled on a school board.       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   allows the governor to make an appointment to fill a vacancy on the State Board of       Education after a member of the State Board of Education submits a letter of       resignation                   allows a local school board to make an appointment to fill a vacancy on the local       school board after a member of the local school board submits a letter of       resignation  and                  makes technical changes.
