An appointment to the committee shall be for a four year term unless the member is       appointed to complete an unexpired term.  The executive director may also adjust the length of       term at the time of appointment or reappointment so that approximately     the committee is       appointed every two years.  The executive director shall annually appoint a committee chair       from among the members of the committee.                 The committee shall meet at the call of the chair, at least three members of the       committee, or the executive director, but no less frequently than once each calendar year.                 A majority of the members of the committee constitutes a quorum.  The action of a       majority of a quorum constitutes the action of the committee.
