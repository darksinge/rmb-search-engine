Bramble             LONG TITLE      General Description             This bill modifies provisions related to classification of crimes and sentencing of       individuals convicted of driving under the influence.       Highlighted Provisions             This bill                   modifies provisions making it a felony for an individual convicted of driving under       the influence while also driving the wrong way on a roadway or controlled access       highway                    modifies sentencing requirements for an individual convicted of driving under the       influence  and                  makes technical changes.
