The board shall ensure that an individual appointed as director possesses              a   outstanding professional qualifications pertinent to the prudent investment of trust        fund money  and              b   expertise in institutional investment management. There is established a School and Institutional Trust Fund Nominating Committee.                  The nominating committee consists of               a   two members appointed by the State Board of Education               b   two members, appointed by the State Board of Education upon recommendation by        the director of the school children s trust section, each of whom is a member of a respected        professional organization               c   the chief investment officer of the University of Utah endowment               d   the chief investment officer of the Utah State University endowment  and              e   the director of the school children s trust section.
