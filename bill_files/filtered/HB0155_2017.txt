              The standards for chemical breath analysis as provided by Section     a     and        the provisions for the admissibility of chemical test results as provided by Section     a            apply to determination and proof of blood alcohol content under this section.                  Calculations of blood or breath alcohol concentration under this section shall be        made in accordance with Subsection     a       .                  The fact that a person charged with violating this section is or has been legally        entitled to use alcohol or a drug is not a defense.                  Evidence of a defendant s blood or breath alcohol content or drug content is        admissible except when prohibited by Rules of Evidence or the constitution.
