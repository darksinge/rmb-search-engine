Bramble             LONG TITLE      General Description             This bill amends provisions of the background check requirements for individuals who       have direct access to children or vulnerable adults.        Highlighted Provisions             This bill                    defines  incidental care                    shortens the automatic denial time frame                   modifies background check exemptions  and                  makes technical changes.
